Aims and Objectives

Established in 1984 by the Nigerian Army
Command Secondary School Abakaliki has the following as its aims and objectives:

  1. To offer qualitative and comprehensive education to boys and girls of Nigerian Army personnel
  2. To attain in its students a triple excellence:
    1. Academic Excellence
    2. Moral Excellence
    3. Service Excellence
  3. Through this qualitative and comprehensive education. CSSA aims at producing young and responsible school leavers. They are to become polished leaders without bias and frontier.

Vision Statement

Our Vision is to raise new breed of Global leaders with intelligence and integrity in a harmonious learning environment who will be change agents in the next generation.
